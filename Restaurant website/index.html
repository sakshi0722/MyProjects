Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors Since 1998

Welcome to Saffron Palace, where culinary excellence meets the warmth of Indian hospitality. Our journey began over two decades ago with a simple mission: to bring the authentic tastes of India to your table.

Every dish we serve is crafted with hand-picked spices, traditional recipes passed down through generations, and a deep respect for the diverse culinary heritage of India. From the royal kitchens of Lucknow to the coastal flavors of Kerala, we bring you India's finest.
